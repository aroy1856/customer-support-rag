ROAMING TARIFF INFORMATION

1. DOMESTIC ROAMING

1.1 WHAT IS DOMESTIC ROAMING?
Domestic roaming allows you to use your mobile services when traveling outside your home telecom circle within India. Your home circle is the state or region where you activated your connection.

1.2 DOMESTIC ROAMING CHARGES
Good news! All our plans now include FREE domestic roaming across India. You can use your plan benefits (data, voice, SMS) anywhere in India without any additional charges.

1.3 SERVICES AVAILABLE DURING DOMESTIC ROAMING
- Outgoing calls: Charged as per your plan (free if within plan limits)
- Incoming calls: Completely FREE
- Data usage: Counted against your plan's data limit
- SMS: Charged as per your plan (free if within plan limits)
- No separate roaming packs required

1.4 DOMESTIC ROAMING ACTIVATION
- Automatic activation for all prepaid and postpaid customers
- No manual activation required
- Works across all telecom circles in India
- Seamless connectivity when crossing state borders

3. ROAMING TIPS AND BEST PRACTICES

3.1 BEFORE YOU TRAVEL
- Activate international roaming 24-48 hours in advance
- Purchase appropriate roaming pack for your destination
- Check coverage in your destination country
- Note down customer care international number: +91-XXXX-XXXXXX
- Enable data roaming in phone settings only when needed

3.2 WHILE ROAMING
- Monitor your usage through mobile app
- Disable automatic app updates and cloud backups
- Use Wi-Fi whenever available to save data charges
- Turn off data roaming when not in use
- Be aware of time zone differences when calling India

3.3 AVOIDING BILL SHOCK
- Set spending limits through mobile app (postpaid customers)
- Check usage daily via app or SMS "USAGE" to +91-XXXX-XXXXXX
- Purchase packs instead of pay-per-use for better value
- Avoid video streaming and large downloads while roaming
