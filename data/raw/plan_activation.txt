PLAN ACTIVATION AND DEACTIVATION POLICY

1. NEW CONNECTION ACTIVATION

1.1 DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
To activate a new connection, customers must provide:
- Valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Driving License, or Voter ID)
- Recent passport-size photograph
- Proof of address (if different from ID)
- Completed Customer Application Form (CAF)

1.2 ACTIVATION TIMELINE
- Prepaid connections: Activated within 2-4 hours of document verification
- Postpaid connections: Activated within 24-48 hours after credit check and document verification
- Corporate connections: Activated within 3-5 business days after agreement signing

1.3 INITIAL CHARGES
- Prepaid: First recharge amount (minimum ₹100)
- Postpaid: Security deposit (2 months' rental) + first month's rental (prorated)
- SIM card cost: ₹50 (may be waived during promotional offers)

2. PLAN CHANGE AND MIGRATION

2.1 CHANGING YOUR PLAN
Existing customers can change their plan at any time through:
- Mobile app: Navigate to "My Plan" > "Change Plan"
- Website: Log in and select "Upgrade/Downgrade Plan"
- Customer care: Call 1800-XXX-XXXX
- Visit any retail store

2.2 PLAN CHANGE EFFECTIVE DATE
- Prepaid to Prepaid: Immediate activation upon recharge
- Postpaid plan upgrades: Effective immediately with prorated charges
- Postpaid plan downgrades: Effective from next billing cycle
- Prepaid to Postpaid: Processed within 24-48 hours after credit verification
- Postpaid to Prepaid: Effective after clearing all outstanding dues

2.3 CHARGES FOR PLAN CHANGE
- No charges for plan changes within the same category (prepaid to prepaid or postpaid to postpaid)
- Prepaid to Postpaid migration: ₹100 processing fee + security deposit
- Postpaid to Prepaid migration: ₹50 processing fee (security deposit refunded after dues settlement)

3. PLAN RENEWAL AND RECHARGE

3.1 PREPAID RECHARGE
- Recharge validity varies by plan (28 days, 56 days, 84 days, or 365 days)
- Recharge can be done through mobile app, website, retail stores, or authorized dealers
- Auto-recharge facility available to avoid service interruption
- Minimum recharge amount: ₹10
- Maximum recharge amount: ₹10,000 per transaction

3.2 POSTPAID BILL PAYMENT
- Bills generated on 1st of every month
- Payment due within 15 days
- Auto-pay facility available for hassle-free payments
- Grace period of 3 days after due date before service suspension

4. SERVICE SUSPENSION

4.1 PREPAID SUSPENSION
- Service suspended when validity expires or balance reaches zero
- Incoming calls and SMS remain active for 7 days after expiry
- Complete deactivation after 90 days of non-recharge
- Number retention possible by doing minimum recharge within 90 days

4.2 POSTPAID SUSPENSION
- Service suspended 3 days after payment due date if bill remains unpaid
- Incoming services blocked during suspension
- Reconnection within 24 hours of payment
- Reconnection charges: ₹100
- Permanent deactivation after 60 days of non-payment

5. VOLUNTARY DEACTIVATION

5.1 TEMPORARY DEACTIVATION
Customers can request temporary deactivation for:
- Travel abroad (to avoid unwanted charges)
- Security reasons (lost/stolen phone)
- Personal reasons

Temporary deactivation process:
- Submit request via customer care or mobile app
- Minimum deactivation period: 7 days
- Maximum deactivation period: 90 days
- Reactivation charges: ₹50
- Monthly rental charges continue during deactivation for postpaid

5.2 PERMANENT DEACTIVATION
To permanently close your account:
- Prepaid: No action needed; number auto-deactivates after 90 days of inactivity
- Postpaid: Submit written request or call customer care
- Clear all outstanding dues
- Return any rented equipment (routers, dongles, etc.)
- Security deposit refunded within 30 days after final bill settlement
- No refund for unused prepaid balance below ₹50

6. NUMBER PORTABILITY (MNP)

6.1 PORTING TO OUR NETWORK
- Send SMS "PORT <10-digit mobile number>" to 1900
- Receive Unique Porting Code (UPC) valid for 30 days
- Visit our store with UPC and documents
- Porting completed within 3-5 working days
- Porting charges: ₹6.46 (including taxes)

6.2 PORTING FROM OUR NETWORK
- Clear all outstanding dues before porting
- No lock-in period restrictions
- Same process: SMS to 1900 and visit new operator's store
- Your account will be automatically closed upon successful porting

7. SPECIAL ACTIVATION SCENARIOS

7.1 CORPORATE BULK ACTIVATION
- Minimum 10 connections for corporate rates
- Dedicated account manager assigned
- Centralized billing and management portal
- Custom plans available based on requirements
- Activation within 5-7 business days

7.2 INTERNATIONAL ROAMING ACTIVATION
- Must be activated before traveling abroad
- Activation request 24 hours before travel
- Activate via mobile app, website, or customer care
- Security deposit may be required (₹5,000 - ₹10,000)
- Automatic deactivation upon return or after 90 days

7.3 DATA CARD/DONGLE ACTIVATION
- Separate connection for data-only devices
- Same documentation as mobile connection
- Activation within 24 hours
- No voice or SMS services
- Can be linked to primary mobile account for unified billing

8. ACTIVATION FAILURE AND TROUBLESHOOTING

8.1 COMMON ACTIVATION ISSUES
- Document verification failure: Resubmit clear copies
- Credit check failure (postpaid): Opt for prepaid or provide additional security deposit
- Technical issues: Contact customer care for manual activation
- SIM card defect: Free replacement within 7 days

8.2 CUSTOMER SUPPORT
For activation-related queries:
- Customer care: 1800-XXX-XXXX (24/7)
- Email: activation@telecom.com
- Live chat on mobile app
- Visit nearest retail store

9. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

All activations comply with:
- Department of Telecommunications (DoT) guidelines
- Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) regulations
- Know Your Customer (KYC) norms
- Aadhaar-based e-KYC for instant activation

10. RIGHTS AND RESPONSIBILITIES

10.1 CUSTOMER RIGHTS
- Choose any plan without discrimination
- Port number to any operator
- Receive activation confirmation within specified timeline
- Get refund of security deposit upon closure

10.2 CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES
- Provide genuine documents
- Inform about change of address or contact details
- Pay bills on time to avoid suspension
- Use services as per terms and conditions
- Report lost/stolen SIM immediately for deactivation
