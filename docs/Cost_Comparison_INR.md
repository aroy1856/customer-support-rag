## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Risks
1. **API Cost Volatility:** OpenAI pricing changes
2. **Adoption Resistance:** Agent reluctance to use AI
3. **Quality Issues:** AI hallucinations or errors

### Mitigation
1. **Cost:** Build contingency buffer (20%), consider self-hosted models

3. **Quality:** Human-in-the-loop, confidence scoring, feedback loops
